The reluctant ninja who only wants to be a normal high school girl.

## What is she good at?

- Very safe on block versus other members of the cast, allowing her to stay aggressive
- Exceptional movement: fastest walkspeed in the game, air fireball (kunai), elusive dash
- Gentle-ish execution via target combos
- High mixup potential: safe buttons let her set up plus-on-block overheads and side switches

## Where is she lacking?

- Low health pool
- Stubby normals, i.e. no long-reaching space control like Ryu 5MK
- Shorter throw range
- Mixup potential comes from EX meter, taking away from super usage
