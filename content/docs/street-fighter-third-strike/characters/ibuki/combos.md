Ibuki is a bit different from the cast where several of her core combos are target combos into specials.

## Day 1 Combos

The LP-MP-HP target combo gives you the world as a new player.

|Combo|Stun|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|c5LP > 5MP > 2HK|18|20|Easy access to sweep|
|c5LP > 5MP > 5HP xx 63214HP|25|38|"Get off of me" combo|
|c5LP > 5MP > 5HP xx 214HK|23|37|214HK leaves you close for strike/throw/overhead mix|
|c5LP > 5MP > 5HP xx 236LP|27|34|You can probably do some funny air kunai stuff on this knockdown|
