For Street Fighter players, Wagner's starter combos feel somewhere between Cammy and Honda: spiral arrow-esque 236, headbutt-esque 214, dp motions, minor juggling, and a little SF6 spice with down-down inputs for installs.

## Terminology

jc = Jump Cancel

dl = Delay

(#X) = Hold

## Day 1 Combos

The missions in UNI 2 are very decent for just starting out. You can find "real" combos on the Miizumi wiki or on YouTube later on.

### True beginner mission combos

Use these two and the 5A Mash into super as you start exploring vs opponents. You'll be spending more time learning to function in neutral.

|Combo|Notes|
|-----|-----|
|2A > 2B > 5C > 2C|The true beginner day 1; pure buttons, low starter into knockdown.|
|5A > 5A > 5A xx 214A|Your "I caught my opp with my fastest button" combo.|
|2A > 2B > 2C xx 214[B]|If you're just starting you can omit the hold, but holding gives you oki. You can swap 214B for 214A for ease.|

### Core mission combos you'll use when mashing with friends

|Combo|Notes|
|-----|-----|
|2A > 2B > 2C > 3C > jc jB > jC > j2C|The better pure buttons day 1, requires familiarity with jump cancels.|
|6B > 6C xx 214B > 236A~5A|6B is a two-hit normal so you have time to do the 6C. The 5A after the 236A is a follow-up that sideswaps.|
|2B > 5C xx 214A > 5B > 6B xx 214[B]|Your first real combo.|
|66C xx 214[B] > 66C > 2AB|Your first dash low yeet.|
|2A > 2B > 2C xx 214B xx 41236D|Day 1 low starter > special > super.|
|2B > 5C > AB xx 41236D|First exposure to steer ender. Low > button > steer ender xx super.|

### Slightly better mission combos

|Combo|Notes|
|-----|-----|
|2A > 2B > 2C > 3C > jc jB > jC > j2C > 66C > 2AB|Extension of the "better pure buttons day 1" combo.|
|6B > [5C] > 6C > dl.2C > 3C > jc jB > jC > j2C > 66C > 2AB|This is where your damage starts becoming "real".|
|2B > 5C > 6B > 6C > dl.2C > 3C > jc jB > jC > j2C > 66C > 2AB|The combos grow.|
|2A > 2B > 2C > 5C > jc jB > jC > j2C > 66C xx 623A > 22C|Evolution hybrid of the two pure button day 1s with a dp and a link.|
