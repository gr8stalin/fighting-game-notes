## LK Tatsu

LK Tatsu (214LK) universally leads to dashup LP Hashogeki (214LP). If done on time, LP hasho will hit meaty and if blocked, leave Ryu +2. Dashup LP hasho can be perfect parried if the opponent sees you doing it a lot, so proceed with caution.

On hit:
 - DR 5MP xx 214MK
    - This creates the exact same setup from 214LK where you can do 214LP as a meaty again

On block:
  - Throw (fwd + throw if not directly on top)
  - 2LK > 5LP (> 5LK if they're passive)

## Heavy DP

Since 623HP is +37 following the S3 balance patch, Ryu can do dash > 6HP meterlessly as oki.

On hit:
  - 2MP xx 214LK
    - Set up 214LK oki and its sequence
  - 2MP xx 623HP
    - Loop HP DP oki

On block (+3):
  - Throw (if point blank, otherwise can try fwd + throw)
  - 5MP > 5LP (xx 214LP)

If your opponent is delay teching on wakeup, you can drive rush after 623HP and hit 5MP.

On hit (+11):
  - 5HP xx special

On block (+3):
  - Throw
  - 5MP frametrap

## MK Donkey Kick

236MK will come up a lot in your single hit confirms and BNBs, and its frame data gives Ryu a very strong fireball drive rush. After 236MK hits opponent, do 236LP and check the opponent's rise: if they quickrise, drive rush after it, but dash -> drive rush if they backrise. Some drive rush options:

- 5HP
  - +8 on hit, +2 on block
    - On hit, frame-tight combo uses 2MK, more lax combo uses 5LK
    - On block, throw/shimmy/light jail
- Throw
  - If opponent is willing to take throw or is parrying a lot
  - Whiff jab
    - Bait reversals
