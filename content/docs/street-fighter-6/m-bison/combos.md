M. Bison's combos take a little getting used to, but once practiced they'll feel very natural.

## Day 1 Combos

Day 1 combos can take you from Rookie to Master so long as you can keep opponents out of the air with 2HP, jMP-MP, and [4]6LP.

### Meterless BnBs

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2LP/2LK/5LP > 5LP xx 236LK|+2|1100|If they block the whole sequence, you're -5 but if you space it well there isn't a 5f in the game that'll hit you|
|2LP > 2LP > 5LK xx [4]6LP|+24|1470|5LK is a link and not a cancel, so the opponent can mash, but 5LK also helps guarantee charge time.|
|5MP > 2MP xx 4-6MP|+24|2320|Carry, damage, and oki.|
|5MP > 2MP xx 214MP|+20|1840|Plants the bomb, but no oki.|
|2MK xx 236MK|+3|1220|Meterless strike-throw situation.|

### Win condition combo

This is a special mention for M. Bison in particular because 236KK allows him to link into 214HP anywhere on screen. This strength of backfist combo gives good oki and plants the bomb, which is his win condition.

|Combo|Advantage|
|-----|---------|
|M/H xx 236KK > 214HP|+31|

This button > OD special cancel makes M. Bison pretty meter efficient at a novice level, allowing Bison to spend 2 bars on this versus 3 bars for a drive rush cancel.
