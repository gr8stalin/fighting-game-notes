Similar to Juri, Mai has relatively easy combos outside of flame stock optimal ones.

Mai doesn't really have traditional combos in the Street Fighter sense: characters like Ryu will have medium > medium xx special BnBs. Mai is reliant on single hit confirms, counterhits, target combos, and drive rush cancels to have "real" combos.

## Day 1 Combos

Day 1 combos can take you from Rookie to Master. Since her 5MP, 5HP, 2MP, and 2MK all cancel into 236K and 5MP is realistically hitconfirmable, great way to practice single hitconfirms and screen control.

### Meterless BnBs

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2LK > 2LP > 2LP xx 236LK|+27|1130|You will be doing this a lot|
|2LP > 5LK.LK.LK xx j214P|+41|1620|This too, but sometimes just the 5LK as the starter.|

### Basic Drive Rush Cancel

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2MK DRC > 4HK > 2MP xx 214HP > 236LK|+27|2072|S3 change, doesn't get meaty 4HK anymore but Chun-esque strike/throw.|
|2MK DRC > 4HK > 2MP xx 214HP > 236MK|+4||S3 change I didn't keep track of, she sacrifices damage to be +4 for strike/throw.|

### Meterless punishes

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2MP > 5HP xx 214HP > 236LK|+30|2640|DRev punish, works anywhere|
|2MP > 4HK.HK > 623MK|+30|3030|DRev punish, corner only|
|5MK > 5HP xx 214HP > 236LK/623HK|+30|2760/3000|DP punish. Choose 236LK for corner carry, 623HK for damage.|
|5HP xx 214HP > 236LK|+31|2560|Counter DI punish. You can dash twice after the DI for corner carry, easier to do one dash.|
