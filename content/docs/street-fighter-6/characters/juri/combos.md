Juri has relatively easy combos outside of Super 2 extensions.

## Day 1 Combos

Day 1 combos can take you from Rookie to Master.

### Meterless BnBs

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|Light > 5/2LP xx 214LP|+37|960|Basic light into fuha store. You will be doing this a lot.|
|Light > 5/2LP or 5LK xx 623LP|+37|960|Basic light into oki/super 3. Hitting ch 2LK will let you combo 5LK to check basically anything.|
|5MP > 2MP xx 214MK|+37||Basic medium > medium > oki special.|
|2MK xx 214MK|+37||Basic low > oki special.|

### Meterless punishes

Juri has varied punishes due to Fuha-enhanced specials, but these are specifically meterless (outside of DI) and Fuha-less punishes that rely more on basics and the system.

Similar to Ryu, I've noted places where you *can* slip in meter usage if you feel comfortable doing so.

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|DI > 2HP xx 236MK > 5LK xx 623HP|+26|2730|Optimal counter DI combo. If the spacing looks weird, swap 2HP with 5HP, sacrifices 100 damage for consistency.|
|Wallsplat > 2HP xx 214HP > 623HK|+37|1888|Blocked DI wallsplat combo.|
|6HP > 5MP xx 623HP|+28|2760|Super easy DP/Super punish, but sacrifices damage and Fuha generation. Swapping 623HP for 214KK gives a Fuha stock and access to all 3 supers. Swapping 623HP with 5MP.4HP.5HP and linking into Super 1 is damage optimal.|
|5MP > 5HP xx 236MK > 5LK xx 623HP|+28|2780|Universal punish on anything that's -6 or more on block.|
