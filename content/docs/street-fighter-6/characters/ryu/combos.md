Ryu's combos are pretty straightforward and don't require too much input nuance. 

## Day 1 Combos

Day 1 combos can take you from Rookie to Master.

### Meterless BnBs

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2LK/2LP/5LP > 2LP/5LP xx 214LK|+35|1070|Technically you can get 3 lights but being more conservative with 2 is safer.|
|5MP > 2MP xx 214LK|+35|1920||
|5MP > 2MP xx 623HP|+37|2320||

### Basic Drive Rush Cancel

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|2MK DRC > 5HP > 4HP > 214LK|+35|1975|Try to go for this one when first starting out with Ryu.|
|2MK DRC > 2HP xx 214HP > 214MK|+26|2026||

### Meterless punishes

|Combo|Advantage|Damage|Notes|
|-----|---------|------|-----|
|DI > 6HP > 2MP xx 623HP|+37|2520|This is your counter-DI combo.|
|Wallsplat > 5HP xx 214MP > 623HP|+23|2176|Blocked DI wallsplat combo.|
|PC 5MP > 5HP > 214MK|+23|2320|DRev punish focusing on corner carry and damage.|
|PC 5MP > 4HP > 236HK|+45|2560|Optimal DRev punish, but 4HP can fall out if you're too far away.|
|5HK > dash > 2HP xx 214LP > 2HK|+39|3070|Best meterless DP punish. If you feel comfortable, replace sweep with 236236HP for more damage.|
