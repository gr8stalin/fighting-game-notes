Juri has several "magic numbers" for her oki versus situations like Ryu LK Tatsu that allow for you to compartmentalize her oki.

## Magic Oki Numbers

Juri's most common oki numbers are **27**, **28**, and **37**. There are situations where she does a combo that leaves her off by one, but it's not a huge deal.

**+27** comes from any combo ending in 214HK > heavy DP, which is her 2MK DRC combo.

**+28** comes from any of the four basic fuha-less punishes where her heavy DP is getting all of its hits.

**+37** is from anything ending in 214LK/MK.

All three of her magic numbers let you do drive rush 5MP. Dashing on Juri for oki is overrated and almost useless. This is really all the oki you need.

On block, dr.5MP is +6. You get free strike into 2mp and get to introduce gaps with 5hp. On counterhit it's +13 since you're still drive rush enhanced, which gives you the world.
