Juri is the drive rush character of Street Fighter 6 where all it takes is a drive rush or 2MK DRC to snowball her way to victory. She's a very versatile all-rounder similar to Chun but with a stronger focus on aggression. Her gimmick is "Fuha stocks" that enhance all of her 236K specials so there's slight resource management, and her 214214P super is an install that enhances her frame data and juggle potential.

## What is she good at?

Juri is very easy to pilot in neutral, with a suite of relatively fast normals that are difficult to contest:

- 5LK is good at controlling space offensively and defensively, and she has good defensive mashing on 5LP and 2LP.
- 5MP is the only +2 on block medium normal in the game, 5MK is an exceptional poke, she has the longest cancelable 2MK in the game, and 2MP is a godlike defensive button because it checks all aggression (even antiairs).
- 5HP is "what if Chun's 5HP was 10f startup, special cancelable, and just as safe". 5HK's advancing reach and slow startup can auto whiff punish opponents if you get a read on their rhythm.

Juri doesn't have any truly glaring faults in her kit and is very feature complete - 2MK DRC 2LP tick throw, DP antiair, reversal DP, throw loop, anti-fireball/aa divekick. Combined with easy neutral and a top-tier drive rush, her basics will get you past many players. Once you get more experience, her Super 2 opens up a lot of combo routes, trickery, and dubious pressure.

## Where is she weak?

Juri is lacking in good 5 frame punishes. Both of her medium punches are 6f startup, and her 5MK must be canceled on the first frame which means any extra minute spacing can result in absolutely nothing.

Fuha stocks aren't difficult to get and most Juri players use ex specials as a bandaid for their decision making, but she does have to manage a (singular) resource. She'll have to choose between oki and fuha stocks occasionally and will have to be aware of fuha stocks for combos, but this can put a stop to her aggression in a game where aggression is king.

Because Juri is the drive rush character, she has the same kind of meter hunger that Chun does where she relies on DRCs to set up some of her best oki and ex specials for quick reaction confirms under pressure.
