## Release year tech (2023)

{{< youtube rY1keZTWkf0 >}}

Nemo talks about dealing with parry and hold parry when your opponent is cornered. It's best to check it with 2mk or 2mp because if you hit the parry they get back about 1 drive bar, but they're still stuck in the corner without doing an obvious drive rush.  If the opponent holds and releases parry, there are 29 recovery frames where the opponent can only block, and this situation allows for a free DI wallsplat.

{{< youtube pWJa3XPVtU >}}

The general public's introduction to drive rush jab in 2023. Three major uses:

- DRJ to punish a reactionary button to the green flash
- DRJ to throw/shimmy
- DRJ to bait a reversal

{{< youtube R6UpZx0j_YI >}}

When landing a perfect parry, some characters benefit from doing drive rush button instead of button > metered special for extended corner carry and meter efficiency.

{{< youtube lL0aCEi1tXw >}}

Zaferino goes over the magic numbers in SF6:

0:
  - "Neutral" on block
  - Both players recover at the same time
  - Creates a "trade" situation where both players pressing a 4f will both receive damage

+1:
  - Your fastest light will be the fastest light in the game and beat any non-invincible reversal your opponent tries to do
  - Throws (5f startup) will lose to 4f buttons

+2:
  - Frame trap: throw beats 4f normals

+3:
  - Creates a frame trap like +2, but characters with slow backdashes (anything above 23f) cannot punish the whiffed throw at +3 on block
  - Avoid reversal perfect parry

+4:
  - Allows for throw looping
  - Frame 1 meaty 4f
  - Most advantage you can have from blockstun that lets you immediately throw

+5:
  - Maximum advantage for oki into throw

+6:
  - Immediate throw will not work on wakeup

+11:
  - Add +11 to whatever you want to drive rush punish with to figure out if you can use a certain normal to punish heavily minus specials

+15:
  - Fastest drive rush into attack possible (11f from before into a 4f)

+22:
  - This is where you can DI trade with 4 frame buttons

+23~27:
  - DI will frametrap lights

+31~34:
  - Drive rush overhead frame traps, allowing you to goad your opponent into spending meter on ex reversal or get them to parry

+42:
  - Safejump vs 5f reversal

+44:
  - Empty jump into +1
